2026 Football Fixtures Widget shows the 2026 international football tournament fixture list with schedule and score-style home-screen widgets.

The app updates once per day from the public openfootball/worldcup.json data file and falls back to bundled CC0 fixture data when offline.

This is an unofficial fan app. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by FIFA, the tournament organizers, or any football federation.

Features:
- Daily schedule refresh
- Bundled fallback schedule
- Fixture list
- Today view
- Results-ready view
- Schedule widget
- Compact score/next-match widget

The app contains no ads, no analytics, no account system, and no proprietary SDKs.

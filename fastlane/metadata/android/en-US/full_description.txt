FIFA WorldCup 2026 Fixture Widget shows the FIFA World Cup 2026 fixture list with schedule and score-style home-screen widgets.

The app updates once per day from the public openfootball/worldcup.json data file and falls back to bundled CC0 fixture data when offline.

Features:
- Daily schedule refresh
- Bundled fallback schedule
- Fixture list
- Today view
- Results-ready view
- Schedule widget
- Compact score/next-match widget

The app contains no ads, no analytics, no account system, and no proprietary SDKs.

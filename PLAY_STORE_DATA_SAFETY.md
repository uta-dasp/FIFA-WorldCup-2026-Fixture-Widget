# App Store Safety Notes

The app is designed to be simple to review for F-Droid and compatible with Google Play safety expectations.

## Data Collection

The app does not collect personal or sensitive user data.

## Data Sharing

The app does not share user data with third parties.

## Network Activity

The app has no network permission and does not make network requests.

## Permissions

No runtime permissions are requested.

## Security Practices

- No ads
- No analytics SDK
- No account creation
- No location access
- No contacts access
- No camera or microphone access
- No device identifiers
- Cleartext traffic is disabled
- Backups are disabled

## Bundled Data

The FIFA World Cup 2026 schedule is bundled from `openfootball/worldcup.json`, which is published under CC0-1.0 / public domain dedication.
